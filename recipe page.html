Anitha's Yippee

This Yippee recipe has been favourite recipe in my family.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Yippee noodles
  • 1 ½ cups of water
  • Yippee tastemaker (comes with the packet)

Preparation

  • Boil Water: Heat 1 ½ cups of water in a pan.
  • Add Noodles: Break and add the noodle cake to the boiling water.
  • Add Spice Mix: Stir in the tastemaker spice mix.
  • Cook: Simmer for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Serve: Once cooked, let it sit for a minute and enjoy!
